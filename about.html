Our Story

Building Excellence Since 2024

S&R Engineering and Renovation PTE LTD (UEN: 202432744G) was founded with a simple mission: to provide Singapore with honest, high-quality engineering and renovation services. What started as a small team of passionate engineers has grown into one of the most trusted names in the industry.

Today, we've completed over 500 projects across Singapore, from HDB flats to commercial spaces. Our success comes from a simple philosophy: treat every project as if it were our own home, communicate transparently, and never compromise on quality.
