Can I live in my home during renovation?
For minor works (painting, plumbing repairs, small electrical), yes. For major renovations, we recommend temporary accommodation due to dust, noise, and disruption. We can help you plan accordingly and minimize inconvenience.

Do I need HDB approval for renovations?
For HDB flats, certain works require approval:
  • Approval needed: Wall hacking, floor replacement, pipe rerouting
  • No approval: Painting, cabinet installation, minor repairs
We handle all necessary HDB applications for you and ensure 100% compliance.
